# Recommended Venues for Invasive BCI Neural Decoding

A submission / tracking guide for researchers working on invasive BCI neural decoding — a field spanning **computer science / AI, neuroscience, biomedical & neural engineering, clinical medicine, and electronics / materials (electrodes)**.

- **CCF** = China Computer Federation recommended-venue rank (A / B / C). Source: official CCF directory — verify against the latest list at <https://www.ccf.org.cn> for your cycle, as ranks are periodically revised.
- **THU** = Tsinghua University CS-discipline recommended list (A / B).
- Ranks are **left blank** where they do not apply (most neuroscience / biomedical / clinical venues are outside the CCF/THU CS lists) or where we are not certain — we intentionally do not guess.

> ⚠️ The CCF/THU columns only meaningfully rank CS/AI venues. Neuroscience, biomedical-engineering, and clinical journals are world-class in their own fields but simply are not in those CS lists — a blank means "out of scope," not "low quality."

## A. Conferences

### A.1 AI / ML / Signal-Processing
*Where decoding-algorithm, representation-learning, and neural-signal-modeling contributions land.*

| Conference | Abbr. | CCF | Field | Relevance |
|---|---|:---:|---|---|
| Conf. on Neural Information Processing Systems | NeurIPS | A | ML/AI | Premier venue for neural-data models, latent dynamics, foundation models for spikes. |
| Intl. Conf. on Machine Learning | ICML | A | ML/AI | Core ML methods for spike/LFP decoding, state-space models, representation learning. |
| Intl. Conf. on Learning Representations | ICLR | A | ML/AI | Self-supervised pretraining for neural time-series, foundation models. |
| AAAI Conf. on Artificial Intelligence | AAAI | A | AI | Broad AI; decoding, time-series, brain-signal classification. |
| Intl. Joint Conf. on Artificial Intelligence | IJCAI | A | AI | Broad AI methods for neural decoding / BCI. |
| IEEE/CVF Conf. on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition | CVPR | A | CV | Visual-cortex decoding, image/video reconstruction from neural activity. |
| Intl. Conf. on Computer Vision | ICCV | A | CV | Visual reconstruction and stimulus decoding from cortex. |
| European Conf. on Computer Vision | ECCV | B | CV | Visual-decoding / reconstruction. |
| ACM SIGKDD Conf. on Knowledge Discovery & Data Mining | KDD | A | Data Mining | Time-series mining, large-scale neural-data analysis. |
| Annual Meeting of the ACL | ACL | A | NLP | Speech/language decoding from cortex (brain-to-text). |
| Conf. on Empirical Methods in NLP | EMNLP | B | NLP | Language/speech decoding, brain-to-text modeling. |
| IEEE Intl. Conf. on Acoustics, Speech & Signal Processing | ICASSP | B | Speech/Signal | Neural-signal processing, speech-BCI pipelines. |
| Conf. of the Intl. Speech Communication Association | INTERSPEECH | C | Speech | Speech decoding / synthesis from neural signals. |

|---|---|---|---|
